MT. SAN VICENTE

If you're searching for a challenging hike and a refreshing escape, Mt. San Vicente is one of the best places to visit. This can be found in the Dupax Watershed Forest Reserve (DWFR), which is managed by DENR and is located in Dopaj, Dupax del Sur. Once you arrive, you'll notice that your 350-steps walk will take you to a breathtaking sight of diverse trees and scattered clouds. And they are merely perks on top of the other lovely things you can get from there. Getting the gift of relaxation and relief all at once.
