ABOUT DUPAX WHERE TO GO EVENTS AND CULTURES REMINDERS

PALINGALINGAN FESTIVAL

The Panlingalingan Festival, celebrated every April, promotes diversity, engages people in conversation, fosters creativity, fosters civic pride, and improves our overall psychological well-being. In short, it improves the quality of life in Dupax del Sur.

DUPAX DEL NORTE TOWN FIESTA

Dupax del Norte's culture is well represented through its festival. More people meeting new people, trying new foods, and coming up with new ideas. They celebrate to live out the universal acquiescence to the community as a whole, for some exceptional event and in a unique manner.

DUPAX DEL NORTE CHRISTMAS VILLAGE

The brilliant and fascinating lights that illuminate the Dupax del Norte Municipal Hall during the Christmas season represent the birth of the world's light, Jesus Christ. The village's picture-worthy decorations entice local tourists to visit.
