SAINT VINCENT FERRER PARISH CHURCH

+63 975 323 5070

San Vicente Ferrer Parish Church, also known as Dupax Church or Dupax del Sur Church, is an 18th-century Baroque church in BarangayDopaj, Dupax del Sur. The church, which is made of brick, lime, coral, or river rock, and stucco wood, has a total size of 7,200 square feet. m. Its architectural architecture is similar to that of Tuguegarao City's St. Peter Cathedral, but with less ornamentation and a lower height, as well as no spiral columns or pilasters to support the structure.
